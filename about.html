GET TO KNOW US

Loving God and Loving People

Christian Restoration Ministries International started with a dream by Pastor Emmanuel Eyim-Danquah, a vision to establish a church that radiates the love of God and embraces everyone as family. The dream led him to Accra, Ghana.

Thirty years, thousands of sermons, and countless transformed lives later, we continue to see God's miracles. Witness God's amazing work at CRMI!

The Mission

The mission of Christian Restoration Ministries International is to glorify God, by leading people to know Jesus and the benefits of His family; equipping them as true disciples to serve both in the Church and in the world.

The Vision

To build an impactful church to positively affect lives, restore, stabilize homes, and build families to know the Lord and give them a place of belonging. To plant impactful churches across the world by raising people to become true disciples that will find their place in the ministry in order to disciple others. To affect our communities with The Gospel of Restoration and Benevolence, seeking restoration for the unchurched, homeless, single parents, and the deprived.
