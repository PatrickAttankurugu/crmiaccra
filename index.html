Welcome to Christian Restoration Ministries International, Accra!

We are a congregation committed to being disciples of Jesus, dedicated to bringing light to the world. Here, we welcome everyone, from every walk of life. Whether you seek answers, need solace, celebrate life, or desire to understand Jesus better, you've found your spiritual home. We rejoice in our differences, learn from our hardships, and grow closer to Jesus through His teachings. No matter who you are, no matter where you find yourself in life, we have a place for you.

Need a Helping Hand?

Life can sometimes be challenging, overwhelming, and even painful. But remember, you are not alone in this journey. In times of hardship, difficulty, or despair, our dedicated team of pastors, staff, and volunteers are here to stand by you. We are committed to providing emotional, spiritual, and practical support to those in need.

Our hearts are open. We are ready to listen, understand, and guide you towards a path of healing and hope. Don't let your burden weigh you down. Reach out to us and find the help you need.
